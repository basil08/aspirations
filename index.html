What is Aspirations?

Aspirations is a product of a personal struggle to meaningfully define my identity as a young responsible human. As I thought deeply about the socio-political issues that plague our society, it became evident that there are few outlets that cater to the intellectual needs of a growing body of young educated minds who are desperately struggling to define their identities just like me. Aspirations aspire to kindle the thought processes that occupy the minds of the society, the future leaders who wish to be informed and ask the right questions.

In short, Aspirations represent the aspirations of this society.

What are the values of Aspirations?

Aspirations aims to save humans against the fanaticism of humans and to serve as a torchbearer for meaningful discourse that enables peace, equality, and dignity for all.
